Rainbow lorakeets

The rainbow lorakeets is a medium-sized parrot. The head is deep blue woth a greenish-yellow nuchal collar, and the rest od the upper parts (wings, back an tail) are deep green. The chest is red with blue-black barring. The belly is deep green, and the thighs and rump are yellow wing-bar cotrasts clearly with the red underwing coverts.

There is little to visually distinguish between thesexes; however, to a keen observer of their colouring and behaviour, their dimorphism is readlly apparent.
